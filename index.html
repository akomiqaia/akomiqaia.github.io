About Me

I'm Akaki Mikaia from Georgia. I was born 23 years ago under the sign of Leo. I was grown up in Chkhorotsku* in a huge family. My family and farm environment helped me to develop a massive communal sense and also taking care of my surroundings (by that I mean how to grow different trees, vegetables or take care of animals). The life changed when I moved to Tbilisi, capital of Georgia, for studies. In Tbilisi, I came to a realization of what technological and industrial development can bring to the society and how it brakes(or replaces) connections among people. one night, just discussing the topic my friends and I decided to start a project that would raise community awareness in our hometown, especially among in young people. The best way seemed to increase intellectual material and introduce different ideas from all over the world. In our hometown libraries resources were not enough to buy books that young people would read and discuss afterward.


My friends and I started a project for the local library. We were buying books for a local library every month. All the new books were represented by us and it became a platform to introduce new ideas to young people. The budget for the project was founded by local authorities, but in a few months, it also raised a good amount of donations from local businessmen. The project has been a real communal achievement that united the people of the small town. I stayed on the project for two years and now the project is managed by a really nice person from the town who enabled the project to go beyond the town and to cover a few surrounding villages. That was the beginning of understanding my philosophy of life.


My philosophy of life can be described as deconstructionist. This way of thinking means to deconstruct or remove centers and help marginal areas to become more visible, but not for the sake of replacing the center. It's a state where marginal and center starts co-existing together in one harmonious circle. At the moment my aim is to help the new world to reach this harmonious state where everything is decentralized and spread around, like a Rhizome. I believe that is achievable by helping local businesses and startups, or building environments that are free and easily accessible.


I strongly believe that Founders and Coders training can give me enough skill to take adventures and help bigger communities to become harmonious with surroundings and with itself as well.
