About Me

I'm Akaki Mikaia from Georgia. I was born 23 years ago under the sign of leo. Me and my friends start a project for a library. We were buying books for a local library every month. All the new books were represented by us and it became a platform to introduce new ideas to young people. The budget for the project was founded by local authorities, but in few months it also raised good amount of donations and private transactions from local bussinesmen. The project has been real communal achievement that united the people of the small town. I stayed on the project for two years and now the project is managed by one of the teachers from the town.
