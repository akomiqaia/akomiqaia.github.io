About Me

I'm Akaki Mikaia from Georgia. I was born 23 years ago under the sign of Leo. I grew up in Chkhorotsku* in a huge family. My family and rural environment helped me to develop a strong communal sense. It also taught me how to take care of my surroundings; How to grow different trees, vegetables or take care of animals. My life changed when I moved to Tbilisi (the capital city of Georgia) to study at university. In Tbilisi, I realized how technological and industrial development can break (or replaces) connections among people.


One night, just discussing the topic, my friends and I decided to start a project that would raise the community awareness in our hometown, especially among young people. The best way seemed to increase intellectual resources, so that we could introduce new and different ideas from all over the world. To acomplish that, we started a project to provide the local library with brand new books and other materials, given its poor situation at that time. We were buying books for the library every month. We, then, were presenting the new materials and organizing talks and discissions about them. All this evolved and became a platform to introduce new ideas to young people. The project has been a real communal achievement that united the people of the small town. Now, the project has grown and its got adopted by the neighbouring villages. That was the beginning of understanding my philosophy of life. That was the beginning of understanding my philosophy of life.


My philosophy of life can be described as deconstructionist. This way of thinking means to deconstruct or remove centres and help marginal areas to become more visible, but not for the sake of replacing the centre. It's a state where marginal and center starts co-existing together in one harmonious circle. At the moment my aim is to help the new world to reach this harmonious state where everything is decentralized and spread around, like a Rhizome. I believe that is achievable by helping local businesses and startups and by building environments that are free and easily accessible.


I strongly believe that Founders and Coders training can give me enough skill to help communities to become harmonious with surroundings and with itself as well.
